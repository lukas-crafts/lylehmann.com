Reimagining Museum Experiences

The Fashion Heritage Museum App project addressed critical challenges in museum visitor engagement through a collaborative and innovative design approach.

Design Challenge

Create a digital solution for museum visitor engagement

Project Focus

Enhancing museum interaction through thoughtful design

Core Objective

Improve visitor experience with digital innovation

Key User Challenges

Information Overload

Museums often overwhelm visitors with too much information

Navigation Complexity

Visitors struggle to understand exhibit layouts

Limited Interaction

Minimal engagement with exhibit content

The Journey

Our approach to solving museum experience challenges

Team Research

Conducted interviews and surveys with museum visitors

Collaborative Ideation

Brainstormed and created low-fidelity wireframes together

Mid-Fidelity Prototyping

Developed shared prototype to validate core concepts

Design Divergence

Team split to create two unique high-fidelity versions

Prototype Exploration

Two unique approaches to museum digital experiences

Mobile App Concept

Personalized, portable museum guide for individual visitors

Museum Touchscreen Version

Interactive in-museum digital interface for exhibit exploration

Shared Design Principles

Consistent user experience across different interfaces

Accessibility Focus

Inclusive design for diverse user needs

Project Insights

Learning from our design exploration

Why did we split into two versions?

To explore different design solutions and learn from diverse approaches to the same problem.

What was the biggest design challenge?

Creating a digital experience that enhances, not replaces, the physical museum visit.

What did we learn?

The importance of user-centered design and collaborative problem-solving.

FocusBuddy - A Safe Space for Focus and Accountability

Overview

FocusBuddy is a macOS app designed to help individuals with ADHD and autism stay focused, accountable, and connected. The app provides a safe space for users to structure their work, find focus-enhancing music, and connect with an online community for support and encouragement.

This case study explores the journey of designing FocusBuddy, from identifying key challenges faced by neurodivergent individuals to turning ideas into reality. By combining personal insights with extensive research, I crafted a productivity app that addresses the unique needs of its users.

Finding Focus in the Chaos

FocusBuddy was born out of my own experiences as a neurodivergent individual. Like many with ADHD, I often found myself struggling to maintain focus, manage my time, and stay productive.

The Journey

Building FocusBuddy was a deeply personal process.
I used the Design Thinking Methodology to guide each step.

Empathize

- Mapped my own struggles and combined them with the stories shared in interviews and surveys
- Learned about tools users tried and what didn't work for them

Define

- Created personas like The Overwhelmed Worker and The Isolated Achiever to capture the range of needs
- Defined problem statements to focus on designing for flexibility and support

Ideate

- Customizable Pomodoro timers to structure focus
- Accountability partner matching for real-time encouragement
- Focus-enhancing music playlists to create the right environment

Prototype & Test

- Developed low, mid, and high-fidelity prototypes
- Collected feedback from neurodivergent users at every stage to refine the experience

Moments That Defined FocusBuddy

Key insights and decisions that shaped the journey

Customization

Understanding that neurodivergent users need flexibility, we prioritized personalized workflows, adaptive dashboards, and visual comfort options including light and dark modes. This customization ensures each user can create their ideal focus environment.

Focus-Enhancing Music

Through testing and personal experience, we discovered the powerful impact of curated music on concentration. This insight led to the integration of a comprehensive focus music library, helping users maintain their productivity flow.

Community Connection

Inspired by body doubling sessions, we built strong community features including accountability partner matching and group focus sessions. These social elements transformed FocusBuddy from a simple app into a supportive productivity ecosystem.

Insights

What I Learned

Discover the key insights I gained while designing FocusBuddy and how they shaped my approach to design.

What was the most important lesson you gained from designing FocusBuddy?

Designing FocusBuddy highlighted the transformative power of empathy in design. By grounding the solution in my own experiences and those of my community, I discovered how to transform shared struggles into meaningful opportunities.

How did this project impact your approach to design?

This project reinforced my passion for creating tools that empower neurodivergent individuals. It showed me the value of understanding users deeply and designing with their unique needs in mind.

What does empathy mean in the context of this project?

Empathy meant truly listening to the challenges faced by neurodivergent individuals, recognizing their unique struggles, and turning those insights into actionable design solutions.

How does FocusBuddy reflect your growth as a designer?

FocusBuddy is a testament to my ability to connect with users on a personal level, leverage research effectively, and translate those insights into impactful design decisions.

## Julio's Digital Makeover: A Content Marketing Case Study

### Project Overview

Julio Cesar Gispert, a Cuban percussionist and teacher in Vienna, sought to grow his music teaching practice while also promoting his performance career. Despite his rich experience, Julio lacked an online presence and a cohesive strategy to promote his dual services. Our team developed a detailed website concept and strategy to enhance his visibility and attract both students and music enthusiasts. However, the project was paused as Julio relocated to Spain.

## Finding Focus in the Chaos

        FocusBuddy was born out of my own experiences as a neurodivergent individual. Like many with ADHD, I often found
        myself struggling to maintain focus, manage my time, and stay productive.

- It wasn't until I discovered **body doubling** -a method where two or more people work together in the same space to stay accountable-that things started to change. I joined digital body doubling sessions and found them incredibly helpful.
- In these sessions, I met others who shared my struggles. Conversations revealed common challenges like distraction, isolation, and the absence of tools designed for our unique needs. This shared experience inspired me to create **FocusBuddy**: a productivity app designed with neurodivergent individuals in mind.

## What We Struggle With

### Learning from Shared Experiences

To understand these challenges more deeply, I combined my personal struggles with input from others:

- Conducted **10 interviews** with neurodivergent individuals
- Surveyed **85 participants** in digital body doubling sessions to uncover patterns and needs

### What I Learned

Designing FocusBuddy taught me the value of empathy in design. By creating a solution rooted in my own experiences and those of my community, I learned how to turn struggles into opportunities. This project reinforced my passion for building tools that empower neurodivergent individuals to thrive.

FocusBuddy is a productivity app concept designed around a single principle: **the interface itself should never become a source of distraction**.

In a world of constant digital interruptions, FocusBuddy provides a structured, calm environment for deep work. Every feature decision was tested against one question: does this protect the user's attention, or compete with it?
